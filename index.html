Who We Are

We are a foremost software company in the veterinary industry, promoting digital growth in the animal healthcare domain in sub-sahara africa with the deployment of Electronic Animal Health Record and Veterinary Hospital Management Software to help animal healthcare centers to optimise healthcare delivery and increase revenue. Our aim is to promote paperless veterinary practices, allow data-driven decision making, enhance client experience, increase patient retention and boost productivity.

Who We Are

We help Animal Healthcare centers and Veterinary Hospitals go digital with our software-as-a service solution. Developed by a team of software engineers and veterinarians, the online and cloud based nature of our software allows its usage in the most remote areas - with limited access to internet infrastructure. Our software is able to manage patient medical record, automate client SMS and email reminder, manage appointments and monitor financial performance.

Cloud-based and Offline-enabled Solution

CyberVet is a cloud-based and offline-enabled software. This means your data are stored on our fa-server and accessed using internet connection. This approach saves you the cost of maintaining a server in your veterinary clinic. However, wether there is internet connection or not, CyberVet is designed to let you keep workflow going. It works when there is no internet connection and synchronises when there is, to provide the full digital experience to the least developed market.

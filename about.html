About Us

We are a foremost software company in the veterinary industry, promoting digital growth in the animal healthcare domain in sub-sahara africa with the development of Electronic Animal Health Record and Veterinary Hospital Management Software to help animal healthcare centers optimise healthcare delivery and increase revenue. Our aim is to promote paperless veterinary practices, allow data-driven decision making, enhance client experience, increase patient retention and boost productivity.

Why Choose Us?

Until now, record and administrative officers in veterinary clinics accross Nigeria typically spend several minutes searching through archive of patient medical files whenever a patient is presented to their clinic. The unpleasant situation that clients are subjected to could be sometimes complicated by avoidable errors in the manual patient identification system. Worst still, veterinarians consume another reasonable amount of time, flipping through dusty pages of poorly recorded medial history.
This paper-based approach to animal medical record and animal hospital management system makes it impossible to automate processes and communications and also perform smart analysis that is critical to providing a top-notch client experience while delivering an optimal patient care service. It is also tedious to generate metrics and statistics that would allow veterinatians to make informed decisions regarding their patients and practise.

Practising With Technology

Our mission is to help animal healthcare centers in Nigeria and accross sub-sahara Africa leverage digital technology and data to improve on animal healthcare services, enhance productivity and increase revenue while promoting a healthy animal and human population.
